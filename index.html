Our initiative


The Youth Mental Health Advocacy Network is a youth-led initiative set to build awareness and advocacy groups across the world to fight the stigma surrounding mental illness and mental health. 


The network was created to create a common space where advocates and those affected by mental illness can have a safe space to share ideas, thoughts and have insightful discussions. 

Our goal is to reach out to as many communities as possible and create a change in the world, one community at a time.
